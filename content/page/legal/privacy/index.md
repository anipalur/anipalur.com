This privacy policy is used to you of how your information is collected and used.

## Consent

By accessing or using this blog ([anipalur.com](https://anipalur.com "Visit my blog.")), you consent to this privacy policy and agree to its terms.

## Information Collection and Use

Your information is collected and used to improve the performance and security of this blog.
**Your information will not be used or shared with anyone except as described in this privacy policy.**

If you contact me directly, I may receive additional information such as your name, email address,
phone number or any other information you may provide.

## Analytics and Log Data

This blog uses [Cloudflare Web Analytics][cloudflare-web-analytics], a privacy-first and lightweight analytics tool that tracks and measures the usage of this blog.
Please read Cloudflare's [privacy policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy "View Cloudflare's privacy policy."),
[GDPR compliance page](https://www.cloudflare.com/trust-hub/gdpr "Learn more about Cloudflare and GDPR compliance.")
and [Web Analytics page][cloudflare-web-analytics] for more.

Whenever you visit this blog, log data that your browser sends is collected.
This data is usually not linked to any personally identifiable information.

This data includes:

- Your computer's Internet Protocol (IP) address and the country you are in
- The type of your device (e.g. desktop, mobile or tablet)
- The operating system of your device (e.g. Windows, macOS or Android)
- The name of your browser (e.g. Chrome, Firefox or Safari)
- The external link or internal page that referred you to a page
- The number of times you visited this blog
- The pages you visited and when you visited them
- Performance metrics of the pages you visited (e.g. the time taken to load a page)

## Service Providers

This blog uses third-party services for the following reasons.

- To provide this blog on my behalf
- To perform blog-related services
- To facilitate this blog
- To analyse how this blog is used

These third parties may have access to your information to perform these tasks.

## External Links

This blog contains references and external links to third-party websites or educational material not explicitly created by me.
These links do not constitute an endorsement or warranty of these websites and are followed at your own risk.
Under no circumstances am I responsible for these sites or their content.

Third-party websites may have different privacy policies and terms of use beyond my control.
Please be sure to check the privacy policies and terms of use of these sites.

Furthermore, articles on this blog may include embedded YouTube videos.
These videos have [privacy-enhanced mode](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/171780#zippy=%2Cturn-on-privacy-enhanced-mode "Learn more about privacy-enhanced mode.") enabled
that prevents YouTube from storing your information unless you play the embedded video.

## Children Under 13

This blog does not address anyone under the age of 13.
I do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
